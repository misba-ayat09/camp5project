Authors

Rupi Kaur

Rupi Kaur (born 4 October 1992) is a Canadian poet,
illustrator, photographer, and author. Born in Punjab, India,
Kaur immigrated to Canada at a young age with her family.

Kiran Desai

Kiran Desai (born 3 September 1971) is an Indian author.
Her novel The Inheritance of Loss won the 2006 Man Booker Prize
and the National Book Critics Circle Fiction Award.

Chetan Bhagat

Chetan Bhagat born 22 April 1974 is an Indian author Five of his novels have been adapted into films.His novels focus on youth and urban India. making him popular among urban audiences

Ruskin Bond

Ruskin Bond (born 19 May 1934) is one of the fellows of the Sahitya Akademi. His first novel, The Room on the Roof, was published in 1956, it received the John Llewellyn Rhys Prize in 1957

Arundhathi Roy

Suzanna Arundhati Roy is an Indian author best novel of her is The God of Small Things, which won the Booker Prize for Fiction in 1997 and became the best-selling book by Indian author.
